Polyscias

💧 Watering: Spray occasionally, water sparingly when top dry, ensure no water pools at bottom
🌡️ Conditions: 18-29°C (>16°C limit), 60-80% humidity
🌿 Care: Medium indirect light, avoid direct sun, clean leaves occasionally
🪴 Soil: 60% coco coir, 20% perlite, 20% bark

Ficus Microcarpa

💧 Watering: Spray occasionally, water thoroughly when top dry
🌡️ Conditions: 18-29°C (>13°C limit), 50-70% humidity
🌿 Care: Bright indirect light, keep away from drafts, clean leaves occasionally
🪴 Soil: 40% coco coir, 30% perlite, 30% bark

Peperomia

💧 Watering: Allow to dry between waterings, water sparingly, water stored in leaves
🌡️ Conditions: 18-24°C (>13°C limit), 40-50% humidity
🌿 Care: Prefers indirect light, remove any yellowing leaves
🪴 Soil: 40% coco coir, 30% perlite, 20% bark, 10% vermiculite

Haworthia Cooperi

💧 Watering: Drought tolerant, water thoroughly when soil is completely dry
🌡️ Conditions: 21-32°C (>10°C in winter), 30-50% humidity
🌿 Care: Bright indirect light, tolerates some direct sun
🪴 Soil: 60% cactus mix, 40% perlite

Gollum Jade

💧 Watering: Drought tolerant, water thoroughly when soil is completely dry
🌡️ Conditions: 18-27°C (>7°C in winter), 30-40% humidity
🌿 Care: Bright light with some direct sun, rotate occasionally
🪴 Soil: 60% cactus mix, 40% perlite
