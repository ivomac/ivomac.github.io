🪴 Plant Collection 🧩 Yes/No Game
Easy Medium Hard Unsorted

A collection of Yes/No Games collected from books and the internet. These are logic puzzles where you ask questions that can only be answered with "Yes" or "No" (or "Unknown/Not Answerable") to a person that has seen the answer beforehand. The goal is to figure out the mistery in each scenario.

Choose a difficulty level, then click on a puzzle title to reveal its question, and then click the question to see the answer.
